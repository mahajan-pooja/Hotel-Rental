Extra Information

Aston at Papakea Resort on Maui's Kaanapali Coast is a relaxing haven on 13 acres of expansive lawns and lush gardens. We offer spacious condominium accommodations overlooking the crystal blue Pacific.
All guest studios, one, two and three bedroom suites at Aston at Papakea Resort come with fully equipped kitchens and in-room washer/dryers for extra convenience. You'll have a private furnished balcony or lanai, where you can relax on your own or with friends and family and enjoy spectacular ocean or garden views. All condominiums are air-conditioned, and complimentary WiFi is offered throughout the resort.

Rules

1. Advanced Purchase/Non-Refundable Rates -
Full prepayment is required for this reservation, your credit card will be charged immediately.

2. Cancellation Policy -
If you cancel for any reason, attempt to modify this reservation, or do not arrive on the specified check-in date, payment is non-refundable. At check in, the front desk will verify your check-out date. Rates quoted are based on check-in date and length of stay. Should you choose to depart early, price is subject to change.

3. Modification of Reservation -
Modifications to your reservation are not permitted. However, a request to cancel an existing Advanced Purchase/Non-Refundable reservation may be permitted if, at the time you are requesting a change, a new Advanced Purchase/Non-Refundable reservation at the same property is booked (subject to availability).
For any modifications/changes to the reservation, you will be charged a change fee of $50.00 USD plus any difference in room charges and applicable taxes. If the new charges are lower than the original reservation, there will not be any refunds. For a cancellation and rebooking to be considered, you must contact the property to request a reservation change no less than three days prior to your scheduled check-in date.

4. Early Departure -
If you depart early for any reason, you will not receive any credit or refund.

5. Extending Your Stay -
Extensions will require a new reservation for the additional date(s). This is subject to availability and prevailing rates, and the existing rate shall not apply.

6. Other Restrictions -
Advanced Purchase/Non-Refundable rates are not combinable with any other offers and promotions and is not available to groups.
